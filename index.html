Delicious Bites Menu

I-Appetizers

  • Caprese Skewers:

    Fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil leaves threaded onto skewers.

    Drizzled with balsamic glaze for a classic Italian flavor.
  • Spinach and Artichoke Dip:

    Creamy dip made with spinach, artichoke hearts, cream cheese, and various cheeses.

    Served warm with tortilla chips or crusty bread for dipping.
  • Stuffed Mushrooms:

    Mushrooms caps filled with a mixture of breadcrumbs, garlic, herbs, and cream cheese.

    Baked until golden and savory.

II-Main courses

  • Spaghetti Bolognese:

    Ground beef or pork cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce with onions, garlic, and Italian herbs.

    Served over a bed of al dente spaghetti and garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Vegetarian Stuffed Bell Peppers:

    Bell peppers filled with a mixture of quinoa, black beans, corn, tomatoes, and spices.

    Baked until the peppers are tender and the filling is savory.
  • Mushroom Risotto:

    Arborio rice cooked with mushrooms, onions, garlic, white wine, and vegetable or chicken broth.

    Finished with Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

III-Desserts

  • Tiramisu:

    A classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese.

    Dust with cocoa powder for an extra touch.
  • Panna Cotta:

    Italian dessert made with sweetened cream, set with gelatin, and flavored with vanilla or other infusions.

    Typically served with a fruit coulis or caramel sauce.
  • Cheesecake with Berry Compote:

    Creamy and rich cheesecake topped with a compote made from mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries).

    The tartness of the berries complements the sweetness of the cheesecake.
